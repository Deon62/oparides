How the App Works

A comprehensive guide to using OPA Rides

Verification Process

All users undergo thorough verification - drivers provide licenses, background checks, and vehicle documents. Car owners verify ownership and insurance. Clients verify identity. Only verified users can access the platform.

Browsing & Discovery

Browse verified drivers and vehicles. Filter by location, price, vehicle type, and ratings. View profiles with photos, reviews, and pricing. Find the perfect driver or vehicle for your needs.

Flexible Payment Methods

Pay securely through the app, directly to car owners and drivers, or with cash. Choose the payment method that works best for you.
